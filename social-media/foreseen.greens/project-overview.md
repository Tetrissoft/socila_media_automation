# Foreseen Greens - Project Overview

## Mission & Objective
To sell superfoods, starting with microgreens. The goal is to provide high-nutrition food to health-conscious individuals and culinary professionals.

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Health-conscious individuals.
- **Secondary:** Chefs, Restaurants, Home Chefs, Cloud Kitchens.
- **Location:** Gurugram, India.

## Vision & Vibe
- **Tone:** Fresh, Energetic, Vibrant.
- **Style:** Modern, clean, but with a "pop" of life and energy.

## Key "Villains" (Problems Solved)
- **Unhealthy Food:** Processed, dead calories.
- **Nutrient Deficiency:** "hidden hunger" despite eating enough calories.
- **Inorganic Produce:** Pesticides, chemicals in standard farming.
- **Low Vegetable Intake:** People not getting enough greens.

## Visual Identity
- **Palette:** Vibrant Greens, Fresh Whites, Energetic Accents.
- **Imagery:** High-definition close-ups of microgreens, dew drops, fresh salads, energetic people.

## Brand Name
**Foreseen Greens**
