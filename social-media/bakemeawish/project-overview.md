# Bake Me A Wish - Project Overview

## Mission & Objective
To create unforgettable memories through exquisite, unparalleled sweet treats that elevate every celebration.

## Location
Lucknow, India

## Target Audience (Multiple Segments)
- **Cake buyers**: Lucknow families, wedding planners, festival celebrators
- **Home bakers**: Aspiring bakers, hobbyists, anyone learning to bake (online class students)
- **College students**: Birthday celebrations, graduation parties, treat-yourself moments, shareable cakes
- **Corporate**: Corporate gifting, office celebrations, team events, Diwali/Christmas bulk orders, client gifting

## Products/Services
1. Handcrafted celebration cakes
2. Online baking classes for home bakers

## Content Pillars
- **Tehzeeb + Premium**: Lucknow's refinement meets handcrafted excellence
- **Festival-Ready**: Diwali, Eid, Holi, Karva Chauth, Valentine's, Christmas
- **Wedding & Milestones**: Custom cakes for life's big moments
- **Local Flavors**: Paan, kesar pista, Awadhi-inspired fusion
- **Baking Education**: Tips, class launches, student success stories
- **College/Student**: Birthdays, graduation, hostel life, treat yourself
- **Corporate**: Gifting, office celebrations, bulk orders, client appreciation
- **Bakery Trends**: Viral formats (burn-away, piñata), trending recipes, what's hot now

## Language
English primary, Hinglish where it fits naturally.

## Avoid
Health, diet, nutrition, wellness messaging. Bakery = celebration + education, not diet advice.

## Vision & Vibe
Warm, Artisanal, Joyful, Premium.

## Key "Villains" (Problems Solved)
Boring Store-Bought Cakes, Artificial Flavors, Forgotten Special Moments.

## Visual Identity
Soft pastels, golden hues, appetizing textures, elegant typography.

## Brand Name
**Bake Me A Wish**
