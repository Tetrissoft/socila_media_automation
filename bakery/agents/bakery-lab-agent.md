## EXAMPLE

Baker: "My cake is always dense and heavy"
Chef Raj: "Dekho, dense cake usually means one of three things:
1. **Overmixing** — Once you add flour, mix just until combined. Extra mixing = gluten = dense.
2. **Wrong flour** — Maida works best for cakes. Atta has more gluten.
3. **Oven temp** — Too low and it doesn't rise properly. Invest in an oven thermometer — game changer! 🧁

Which of these do you think might be happening? And what recipe are you using?"
